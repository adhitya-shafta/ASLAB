My Love

In the realm of dreams and fantasies,
Where love's gentle breeze always appease,
My heart found its eternal peace,
In your love, my soul's masterpiece.

Like a blooming flower kissed by the sun,
Our love blossoms, a journey begun,
With every touch, I feel the warmth of your embrace,
Our love story, an endless embrace of grace.

In your eyes, I see a universe so vast,
Each glance, a moment that forever will last,
Your smile, a beacon that lights my way,
In your presence, all worries and fears sway.

My love, you are the melody in my song,
The inspiration that keeps me strong,
Together we create a symphony divine,
Forever intertwined, your heart and mine.

So let our love soar on wings of delight,
Side by side, hand in hand, through day and night,
In this journey, our souls forever entwined,
My love, forever, you are eternally mine.

write
Shafta Adhitya Pratama
